To say I love chocolate would be an understatement. Leonidas provides the means to satisfy my chocolate cravings whether its in the form of their hot chocolate drinks, their cold chocolate Javalanches and their assortment of truffles and other types of chocolate candy. 

 Leonidas is to chocolate what Rolls Royce is to automobiles. The quality is as high as it gets. Describing their chocolates would not do justice. One needs to experience it first hand.
