LOVE THIS PLACE!! Hipster, comfortable and stylish. Here are the perks:
1. Upon arrival, you receive 2 coupons for 2 cocktails per person, per night stay.
2. Wine tasting from 5-6 in the loungey lobby
3. Mini bar in the rooms are awesome
4. Comfortable beds
5. Cool bar with yummy cocktails
6. Nice adirondak chairs outside your room to chill in
7. Pretty pool and hot tub area
8. Love the fire pits at night
9. Lots of parking
10. Great location right on Scottsdale Rd. 

We also had dinner in Taggia, the resort's restaurant. Service was great; food was also delicious. Overall, the next time we head down to Scottsdale, we'll be staying here!
