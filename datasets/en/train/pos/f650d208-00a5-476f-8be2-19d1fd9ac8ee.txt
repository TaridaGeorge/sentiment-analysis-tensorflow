Update!

If you happen to go and enjoy your experience at Wag N Wash, sign up for their frequent purchasing program. Yeah, you'll get the key fobs and card to use whenever you purchase something. There is one difference, they do send gift / discount cards! The typical card is $10 off a minimum of a $10 purchase. So far in 10 months I have received 3 of them. Great deal that really adds up!
