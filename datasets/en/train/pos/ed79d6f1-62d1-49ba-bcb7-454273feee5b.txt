After reading stellar reviews by my friends Marcus D and Gabi M. I was anxious to check out NOCA. The problem was, it was too popular, and realized the extreme need to make reservations. So, for my birthday as a surprise, my boyfriend made us a reservation for NOCA.

It has taken me a month to write this review, and to sum it up in one, maybe two words "Wow" and "Memorable". I still can't stop talking about it, and regret that I had not yet ben there before.
