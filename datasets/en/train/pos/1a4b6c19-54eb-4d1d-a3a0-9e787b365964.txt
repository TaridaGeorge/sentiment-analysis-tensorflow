Need to get rid of a few old kick-knacks?  Some old clothes or an eyesore of a machine?  No worry, drop it off to Goodwill donation Center. 

The location is extremely convenient, located in the southwest corner of the Fry's center; it's even open until 6 pm on Sundays.   All you need to do is pull up and wait for an attendant to come out to your car.  No hassle.  Need a receipt?  Then you re on your way...
