Moved back to Tempe and am now walking distance from the Cornish, so I've been in about once a week since June.  Sometimes for food, but some times just for ice cold beer and ice cold air in a great atmosphere.  The Lamb Vindaloo is even better than I remember, and discovered the Mexican.  The big new find was their Chicken Tikka Salad (check the pic).  Great if you're looking for something tasty but lighter than a pasty
