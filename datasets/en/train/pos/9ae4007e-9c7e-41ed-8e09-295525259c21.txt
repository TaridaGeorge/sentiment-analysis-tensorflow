I dream about it.
They sneak some amazing stuff in there: okra, pineapple, button mushrooms.  The sauce in incredible.
A dream for vegetarians.
Don't be in a hurry at Sens.  Eat a little.  Eat a little more.  Drink a phenomenal cocktail (so worth the splurge).  Eat some more.
The service is not quick but is always lovely.
The dj's always spin good tunes, though not always of the same genre.  One night seemed to be early 90's hip-hop, another night was like listening to my My Morning Jacket Pandora station.
