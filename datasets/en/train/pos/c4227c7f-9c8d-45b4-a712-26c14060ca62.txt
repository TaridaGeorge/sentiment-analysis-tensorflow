How good is Torched Goodness?

Well, it's a mobile Creme Brûlée operation that began during the worst depression in the United States since the Great Depression and has managed to stay in business. That should be your first clue that something's being done right.

Next, they offer a Salted Caramel Creme Brûlée, as in, crunchy and creamy, juxtaposed, made out of nothing but sugar, salt, and fat. What more could you crave? Nothing, unless you're some kind of sea floor bacterium.

Does it make sense as a concept? Not really. Should that stop you from buying a gourmet dessert from a truck window? Not unless you hate happiness.
