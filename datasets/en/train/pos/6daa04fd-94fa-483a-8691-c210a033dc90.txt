Convenient location to Downtown. Perfect for parents with kiddies in the Saturday morning classes at the Valley Youth Theater. The best feature is the option to buy farm fresh produce from local growers, fresh bread and delicious eats all the while enjoying live music. You can pay with your credit card by getting an invoice for all your items from any vendor. Then take all your invoices to one booth, pay and then go back to pick up all your stuff. There are many handmade crafts, foods, even art work.
