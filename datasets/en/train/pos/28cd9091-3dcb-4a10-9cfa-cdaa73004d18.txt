If you want to support local business this is the place to go. New to Downtown, they are an eco friendly salon that provides a cozy neighborhood feeling. All the products are vegan friendly. The salts and pedicure scrubs are made locally by an energy worker,  the nail polish remover is made from soy, even the blow dryers are locally made.  The employees are very friendly, earthy, and easy going.  If you want a healthy alternative to all those toxic salons this is definitely some place worth trying.
