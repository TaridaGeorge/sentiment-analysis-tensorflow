Easily best breakfast sandwich in the Valley.

They cook their eggs on a hot pan instead of nuking them.

The bagels are equally as good.  And if you eat there, you get to make fun of all the suburban housewives and husbands who come in decked out in their monochromatic workout gear.

Why 5 stars instead of 4?  I have considered driving an hour and 15 minutes roundtrip several times to pick one of these things up.  I was also sober.
