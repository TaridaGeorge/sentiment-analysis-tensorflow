This is, by far, the best sushi restaurant in the Valley. I have eaten in sushi bars all over the world, including Tokyo, and this place is rivals the best of them. The sushi chefs are what you expect sushi chefs to be, Japanese. The presentation is perfect, the staff is friendly; you're greeted by the entire staff when you walk in and receive a farewell when you leave. I have been to this restaurant on numerous occasions and have never been disappointed. Before finding Hiro I spent years trying different sushi bars, and now this is the only one I will go to. 
Tip: try the Uni
