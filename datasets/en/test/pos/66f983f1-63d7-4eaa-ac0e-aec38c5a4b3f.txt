Are you tired of hearing all the hype or getting stuck in line at Pizzeria Bianco?  If so, seek out this hidden gem in Tempe.  The pizza is incredible and are easily some of the best pies in the Valley...oh...and you won't be starving from a 3 hour wait.  Bianco is one of those places you take out-of-towners to ooohh and ahhh at maybe once every year or two...Classic Italian Pizza is the place you keep going back to over and over!
