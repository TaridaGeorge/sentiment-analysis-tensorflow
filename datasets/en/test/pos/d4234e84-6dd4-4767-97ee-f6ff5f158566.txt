If you are looking for the best pastrami and corned beef in the Phoenix area, Miracle Mile Deli is the place.  The food line is super fast, the portions are huge and delicious.  This is a true east coast deli with lots of choices and a great staff.  Try the Straw or the New Yorker and you will be a happy little camper.  You can also get lots of healthy choices or naughty ones like mac and cheese as well.
