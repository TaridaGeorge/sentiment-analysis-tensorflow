Q-to-U-BBQ opened its doors July 1. The new Anthem restaurant is owned and operated by David and Karyn Rosol, with their boys Ryan and Zachary assisting.  Great family restaurant - 

Award-winning BBQers, the Rosols regularly compete in Kansas City Barbecue Society (KCBS) sanctioned competitions under the nationally ranked team Toys 4 BBQ'N. In 2008 they were recognized as the 4th ranked team in Arizona for competitive barbecue.
