Though their creamy rich ice cream is the main draw, they're more of an all-around dessert shop, offering yogurt, sorbet, shakes, cookies, and even cupcakes. 

The proprietor is from the Bay Area, but unlike the many yogurt and sweet shops that have sprouted up around here, there is little competition in the Valley in terms of creating unique flavors--like their Cheese Course Duo or Sumatra Espresso Shot--made from homemade, all natural ingredients.  People have flocked to try something, not only new and different, but good.  They've become a thriving business since opening last summer.
