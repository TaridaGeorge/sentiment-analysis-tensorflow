Step into Mexico when you enter this market- No one speaks English, prices are low, and the food is delicious

* Great selection of Mexican produce, dry goods, and meats (almost any part you can think of)

* For Americans- They have fresh taco shells, tortillas, chips, and all sorts of dips.

* There are a few areas of the store you can eat. Taco Bar, another food bar (including soups), Juice Bar, and Ice cream. 

* Overall: Excellent cultural experience for you and your taste buds. Warning- Come hungry!
