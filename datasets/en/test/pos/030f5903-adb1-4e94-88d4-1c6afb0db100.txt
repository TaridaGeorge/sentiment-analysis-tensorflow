Cheri Cherie Perfumes is an incredible Perfume and cosmetic store located at the Westgate City Center in Glendale,AZ. 

 Largest perfume selection in Arizona hands down. 
 Friendly staff who provide excellent customer service.
 HUGE selection of NYX cosmetics
 Affordable perfumes starting at under $20.00

The store is a class act and there is no doubt the owner spent a fortune to showcase one of the most incredible in-store selections of both men and women's perfumes and colognes. You can tell the owner and management take pride in the store and really want it to be a terrific shopping experience. 

They also have one of the best perfume websites on the net which includes access to thousands of perfumes from designers I've never even heard of. Check them out at; http://www.chericherie.com if you can't make it into this fabulous store.
