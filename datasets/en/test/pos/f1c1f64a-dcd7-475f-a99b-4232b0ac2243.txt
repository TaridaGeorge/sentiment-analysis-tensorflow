Cafe Bink is a tiny gem. The outside patio offers a wonderful view of Black Mountain in Carefree, Az.  The menu offers unique and elegant dishes that excite the palate.  There are a few main staples however, the menu and entrees change with the seasons. The staff are very friendly and enjoy serving people. While eating at Cafe Bink you truly comprehend the fact that the Chef/kitchen is cooking just for you.  Portions are elegantly presented and leave you satisfied - yet allow room for dessert. YUM!
