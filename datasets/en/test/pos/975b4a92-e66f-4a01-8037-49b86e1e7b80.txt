Pies -- what else -- by-the-slice or the whole pie. 

Sweet pies include classics such as fresh seasonal berries, cream pies, and apple; as well as more unique offerings such as Italian Ricotta wth raisins, chocolate, citrus & nuts, or Pear and Pecan Streusel.  

They also make wonderful meat pies and quiche, and gift baskets with pies, homemade cookies, and exotic coffees.
