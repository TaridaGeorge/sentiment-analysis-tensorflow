I love this place. It was worth the almost-passed-it-so-had-to-drive-backwards-on-a-major-street fiasco.

Usually, my favorite cookie is of the chocolate chip variety, but at Simply Bread, the oatmeal cherry pecan rules.
Try the orange-anise challah, or any of the challahs for that matter.
The Funky Monkey is usually great (sometimes has a slight plasticy taste from the bag).
