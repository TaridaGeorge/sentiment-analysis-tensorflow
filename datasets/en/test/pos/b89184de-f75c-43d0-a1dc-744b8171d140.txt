Scottish food & music, Scottish dancing, bagpipe and athletic competitions, and lots of Scottish tartans from many Sottish clans.

The Caledonian Society of Arizona puts on the annual Scottish Gathering and Highland Games.

There are athletic events; piping, drumming and Pipe Band competitions; Highland Dancing exhibition; Tartans, Genealogy and Clans; historical reenactment groups; Scottish country dancing; 2 stages of entertainment; a children's area with games, magicians, and activities; food and beverages; education center; and vendor booths.

$15 cover ( Sat / Sun )

Fun for all!
