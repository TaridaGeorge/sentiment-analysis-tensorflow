The staff here know their stuff and most of the products offered are not marked up. It is really a photography haven. 

I've come in here off and on over the past ten years to buy film, use their processing facility located next door to the camera shop, and to have my trusty ole film SLRs cleaned by the awesome staff. They are good to my family, supporting our shutterbug ways without pushy sales pitches or any negative experiences, which is comforting.

These guys are a great neighborhood shop to have if you have camera or photography (digital or film) related questions and are no nonsense. I am sure they will guide me through transitioning to digital with all the patience, knowledge and professionalism they have always shown when the time comes (to pry my film from my cold, dead, pretentious fingers.
