Wow! Jamba juice is good, no doubt about it. Inta Juice, however, is AMAZING! I really love this place. When I step up to order they know me by name; not because I go in all the time, but because they go out of their way to learn and know their customers! Excellent service! Excellent product! Every smoothie or juice drink I have tried here has been incredible. The delicious variety can sometimes make it difficult to pick just ONE smoothie, but the staff there is more than happy to suggest something they feel you might like. They haven't failed me yet. Love it! I dare you to try to find something there you don't love absolutely.
